Privacy Policy

SynergyForge (“we”) provides gameplay planning tools for Marvel Strike Force players.

Data Sources

Game-related data displayed in SynergyForge is provided “as is” via the official Scopely/MSF API where available.

What We Collect

  • OAuth profile identifiers needed to link your game account (no passwords).
  • App analytics (aggregated, non-identifying) to improve features.

Data Retention

API-provided data is cached for up to 30 days and then refreshed or deleted.

Your Choices

You may request deletion of your cached data via contact@synergyforge.app.

Security

We use HTTPS and reasonable safeguards. Do not share your account credentials with us; we never ask for them.
